Project Reserve is a cultural heritage website designed by BSOA 2B students to promote the importance of the Filipino Culture that the youths specifically are slowly being forgotten.

Explore Us

Who we are and what do we do?

Mission

To acquire, document and preserve, and exhibit the unique physical and cultural history which reflects the story of the Philippines specifically in the Bicol Region.

Vision

Project Reserve will be a resource for students, youth and educators as well as cultural heritage informations that everyone deserve to know.

Advocacy

Culture gives us a sense of our own identity. We all have different beliefs, values, customs, and traditions that enable us to identify and differentiate ourselves from others. Throughout the world, there are varieties of cultures that pass on from generation to generation. Just like in our country, we also have our cultures that show how unique Filipinos are. However, as time passes by, a lot of Filipinos neglected and have forgotten our very own culture. Many have forgotten who they are and where they came from because of the changes brought by different technologies and modernity.

With this regard, we, the youth, must do something to preserve our own culture. Therefore, we decided to advocate for the preservation of our culture.
